## 1. What MomFluence is

MomFluence operates a **sub-affiliate network**. We hold direct affiliate relationships with one or more upstream networks and brands ("Upstream Partners"). We make those offers available to vetted local mom-creators ("Momfluencers") through this platform. When a Momfluencer drives a qualifying click that converts at the brand, the Upstream Partner pays MomFluence, and MomFluence pays the Momfluencer net of the platform margin disclosed in the offer detail (default: ten percent / 10%).

You are an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, or joint-venture relationship.

## 2. Eligibility

- maintain at least one active social channel (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or YouTube) with at least 500 followers,

We reserve the right to decline, suspend, or terminate any account at our sole discretion.

## 3. How payouts work

When you generate a tracking link for an offer in the catalog, MomFluence creates an upstream affiliate URL with your unique sub-ID embedded. When a click on that link converts at the brand, the Upstream Partner reports the conversion to us via postback. We record:

- the **gross commission** (the amount the Upstream Partner pays MomFluence), and
- your **net payout** (the gross commission minus the platform margin shown on the offer at the time you generated your link).

The payout shown on the offer card is the net amount you will earn per qualified conversion. The margin baked into that figure is fixed for the lifetime of any link generated while it was displayed; if MomFluence changes the margin on an offer, only links generated after the change use the new rate.

Conversions are credited as **pending**. When the Upstream Partner approves the conversion (typically within 30 days), the conversion moves to **approved**. **NET-30** from approval, approved conversions are paid out to you in a single batch, subject to a $50 minimum payout balance. If your balance is below $50 at the end of a payout cycle, it carries forward.

We pay via PayPal, Venmo, or ACH depending on which method you select in your profile. You are solely responsible for the tax consequences of payments received; MomFluence will issue a **1099-NEC** at the end of any calendar year in which you earn $600 or more.

## 4. What you must do (creator obligations)

You agree to:

- **Disclose**: every post that mentions or links to a MomFluence offer must include `#ad` or `#sponsored` (or an equivalent disclosure required by the platform you are posting on) prominently — not buried inside a wall of hashtags.
- **Be honest**: only describe offers, brands, or services you have personally tried or are willing to try. Do not fabricate experience.
- **Respect kids**: do not feature any minor (under 13) in identifiable form without that minor's parent or legal guardian's documented consent. Never include the minor's full name or school name.
- **No medical claims**: do not promise health outcomes, diagnose, treat, or cure conditions on behalf of any MomFluence brand. Healthcare-vertical brands are restricted to "I had a great experience" framing.
- **No incentivized traffic**: do not promise anyone payment, prizes, points, or in-kind value in exchange for clicking your link or converting. No incentivized clicks. No reward-wall traffic. No cookie stuffing. No popups or interstitials. No iframe loading.
- **No spam**: do not post the link in unsolicited DMs, Reddit threads, or comment sections of unrelated accounts. Your own audience only.
- **No impersonation**: do not impersonate the brand, MomFluence, or another Momfluencer.
- **Use your own accounts**: only post from social accounts you personally own and operate.
- **Keep credentials private**: never share your MomFluence login.

## 6. What disqualifies a conversion

A conversion may be reversed (moved from `pending` or `approved` to `rejected`) if the Upstream Partner determines, in good faith, that:

- the user did not actually purchase / book / sign up,
- the user requested a refund or chargeback,
- the click was driven by traffic prohibited under Section 4,
- the conversion is a duplicate of another conversion already credited,
- the click came from a user who clicked another affiliate's link more recently in the conversion window (last-click attribution),
- the geographic, demographic, or device targeting required by the offer was not met.

We will reflect the reversal in your dashboard and, if the conversion had already been paid, deduct the corresponding amount from your next payout.
