# Prohibited Content & Channels (v1)

You may NOT promote MomFluence offers in any of the following ways. Violations are immediate cause for account suspension and forfeit of pending earnings.

## Channels (where you post)

- Paid search ads bidding on the brand's name or trademarks.
- Display ads (Google Display, Facebook ads) using the brand's name in the ad copy unless we've explicitly authorized.
- Coupon, deal, or cashback sites you don't own.
- Reward / GPT (get-paid-to) sites.
- Forums and communities you don't actively participate in (drive-by spam).
- DMs to people who haven't opted in.
- Comment threads on other creators' posts where the comment isn't relevant to that creator's content.
- Any platform that prohibits affiliate links in their TOS.

## Content (what you say or show)

- Medical, financial, or legal advice on behalf of the brand.
- "FDA approved", "guaranteed results", "miracle cure", or other unverifiable claims.
- Any minor (under 13) identifiable on camera without parent/guardian consent on file.
- Any minor's full name, school name, or pickup/drop-off location.
- Defamatory or false claims about a competitor of the brand.
- Sexual or sexually suggestive content, even when the brand is in a beauty or wellness vertical.
- Hate speech, harassment, or content that would violate the platform's community guidelines if it didn't have an `#ad`.

## Mechanics (how you drive traffic)

- Cookie stuffing.
- Iframe loading of the destination URL.
- Auto-redirects, typo-squatted domains, popups, popunders.
- Promising payment, points, prizes, or anything of value to the user in exchange for the click or conversion (incentivized traffic).
- Bots, scripts, or automation generating clicks or conversions.
- Buying clicks from a third party.
- Generating self-clicks or conversions.

## Identity

- Impersonating the brand, MomFluence, or another Momfluencer.
- Claiming to be an employee of the brand.
- Operating multiple Momfluencer accounts.
- Sharing your login credentials.

If you're unsure whether something is allowed, email `compliance@momfluence.app` *before* you post.
