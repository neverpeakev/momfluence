# MomFluence Payout Terms (v1)

These Payout Terms supplement (and are incorporated into) the **MomFluence Sub-Affiliate Agreement**. If anything below conflicts with the main agreement, the main agreement controls.

## 1. The displayed payout is your net

Every offer in the catalog shows a **payout** figure (e.g. *"$45 per conversion"*). That figure is what you earn — it already has the platform margin baked out. You don't see the gross.

The platform margin is displayed in the offer details. The default margin is **10%**. Examples:

| Brand pays MomFluence | Margin | You earn |
|----------------------:|-------:|---------:|
| $50.00                | 10%    | $45.00   |
| $40.00                | 10%    | $36.00   |
| $30.00                | 10%    | $27.00   |

If we ever change a margin on an offer, links you generated *before* the change keep their original payout — there is no retroactive change to your in-flight links.

## 2. Conversion lifecycle

```
click  →  conversion (pending)  →  approved  →  paid
                              ↓
                         rejected / reversed
```

| State | What it means | When it happens |
|---|---|---|
| **pending** | The brand has registered the conversion via postback but has not yet validated it. | Immediately on postback. |
| **approved** | The brand has validated the conversion and we have committed to pay you. | Typically within 30 days of the click. |
| **rejected** | The brand reversed the conversion (refund, fraud, dup, attribution bust, etc.). | At any time prior to payout. |
| **paid** | We sent the funds to you. | NET-30 after approval, on the next payout cycle. |

## 3. Payout schedule

- Payout cycles run on the **15th of each month**.
- Conversions move into the cycle if they were marked **approved** at least **30 days** before the cycle date.
- The payout includes **all approved conversions** that meet the 30-day rule and that, summed, are **≥ $50**.
- If your balance is < $50, it carries forward to the next cycle.

Example: a conversion approved on Apr 1 enters the May 15 cycle (approved more than 30 days prior). A conversion approved on May 1 enters the June 15 cycle.

## 4. Methods

You choose your payout method on the Profile page. Supported methods:

- **PayPal** (preferred) — payout sent within 1 business day of cycle, instant arrival.
- **Venmo** — same.
- **ACH** — sent within 1 business day of cycle, 2–3 business days for arrival.
- **Check** — only on request, mailed within 5 business days, $10 processing fee deducted.

If a payout fails (wrong handle, closed account, etc.), the funds go back into your balance and we reach out by email.

## 5. Taxes

You are responsible for all taxes on amounts paid. We require a completed **W-9** before your first payout. If you earn $600 or more in a calendar year, we issue a **1099-NEC** by January 31 of the following year.

## 6. Reversals after payout

If the brand reverses a conversion that was already paid out, the amount is deducted from your next cycle. If the deduction would exceed your balance, you owe us the negative amount; we will email you and may pause your account until it's resolved.

## 7. Disputes

If you believe a conversion was incorrectly rejected or a payout was incorrectly calculated, email `payouts@momfluence.app` with the conversion ID and your reasoning. We'll respond within 5 business days. Disputes are first reviewed by us; if unresolved, they're escalated to the Upstream Partner.
